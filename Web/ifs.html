Image for Success Website

This site is a pro-bono project for the non-profit organization Image for Success. Based in San Rafael, CA, this company provides professional wardrobes to disenfranchised people.The elegant and professional design of the site was

created to attract potential volunteers and people looking for wardrobes. The refined typography and placement of the rich, warm colors against the wood textured background create an inviting feeling.
