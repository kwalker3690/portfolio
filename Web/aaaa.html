Asian American Arts Alliance Website

This site was designed for a New York arts group. Due to the fact that the site was based on the Ning platform, a content management service, the design had adhere to certain structural requirements. The piece of drawing paper that is used

as a background creates an interesting texture that appeals to the artistic clientele of this site. The modern color scheme and clean typography form a uniquely creative feel.
